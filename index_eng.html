Introduction

WatchBird in the World is an e-commerce service platform that is the world's first NFT marketplace where you can trade a variety of life-based contract products.

From culture, art, game characters, fashion, and sports, we will NFT the familiar products we used in reality, so that we can buy and sell ownership or generate profits through joint purchases, and receive identification such as mobile tickets and certificates.
